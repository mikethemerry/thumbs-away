# Chrome Web Store Listing

## Extension Name
Thumbs Away

## Short Description (132 characters max)
Hide thumbs up/down buttons on Claude, ChatGPT, and Gemini to keep your business data out of AI training.

## Detailed Description

Protect your business data from accidentally being flagged for AI training.

When you click feedback buttons on AI chat platforms, your conversation may be reviewed or used to improve their models. For business users handling sensitive information, this creates unnecessary risk. Thumbs Away eliminates that risk by hiding the buttons entirely.
